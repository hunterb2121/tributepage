A Timeline of Her Life:

  • 1815 - Ada Lovelace is born December 10th in London, England
  • 1824 - Ada's father Lord Byron dies in war
  • 1829 - Ada is struck with measles.
  • 1832 - Ada recovers from measles
  • 1833 - Ada meets Charles Babbage
  • 1835 - Ada marries William King
  • 1836 - Ada has her first child
  • 1837 - Ada has her second child
  • 1843 - Ada published a translation of Analytical Engine by Luigi Menabrea. She creates what is basically the first programming language.
  • 1852 - Ada Lovelace dies November 27th in London, England

“The more I study, the more insatiable do I feel my genius for it to be.”

- Ada Lovelace
